Privacy Policy

At Snack Ranger, your privacy matters. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when using our website or services, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, and other identifying data.
  • Business Information: Company name, location, and details related to vending machine services.
  • Payment Information: Payment details provided for transactions, securely processed by third-party providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process orders and deliver vending machine installation, stocking, and maintenance services.
  • Communicate updates, support, and service details related to your account.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use strong security measures including encryption and strict access controls to safeguard your personal data.

4. Sharing Your Information

We never sell or share your personal data with third parties except when legally required or to provide services (e.g., payment processors).

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tech to track activity and enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may be affected.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal limits.
  • Opt out of marketing communications anytime by contacting us.

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated "last updated" date.
