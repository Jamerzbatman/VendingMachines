Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2025

1. Who We Are

Snack Ranger we provides smart vending solutions for businesses across California. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website or mobile pages.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact details – name, email, and phone provided via forms.
  • Business data – company name, address, number of machines, and preferred setup time.
  • Location data – latitude/longitude from device GPS or address autocomplete (optional).
  • Usage data – anonymous analytics on page views and button clicks.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule site walkthroughs and install proposals.
  • To improve our products, services, and mobile experience.
  • To send occasional updates and promotional offers (opt‑out anytime).

4. How We Share Data

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data is shared only with:

  • Our internal Snack Ranger team members who need it to serve you.
  • Trusted service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, email) bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Authorities if required by law.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use lightweight cookies and Google Analytics to measure site performance. You can disable cookies in your browser without losing access to our pages.

6. Your Privacy Choices

  • Request a copy, correction, or deletion of your data anytime.
  • Opt out of marketing emails via the unsubscribe link.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, email privacy@snackranger.com
